  • Act under the supervision of the Consultant and to support the Consultant in their duties.
  • Admit patients from the Emergency Department.
  • Attend daily ward rounds with the team.
  • Take hand over of patients from previous shift Registrar, so that continuity of care is ensured and immediate decisions are made and orders written.

  • Undertake any other duties that may be required, which could include assisting medical colleagues throughout the hospital and acting as a resource for more junior colleagues

  • Minimum 3- Years experience after MBBS
